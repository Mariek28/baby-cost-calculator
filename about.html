About Baby Cost Calculator

Baby Cost Calculator helps parents estimate and organize common first-year baby costs before and after a new baby arrives.

The site focuses on practical newborn budgeting topics, including diaper and formula expenses, childcare, healthcare, nursery setup, baby gear, and recurring monthly costs.

The goal is to make early planning easier with simple pages parents can read quickly and update with their own local numbers.
